Frequently asked questions
Billing, limits, and plan changes.
You pay per Oracle connection per month. Individual is $49/connection, Team is $39/connection + $29/seat, Business is $29/connection + $19/seat. Fewer connections means a lower bill. Add connections as your fleet grows.
One connection = one Oracle database endpoint. RAC clusters count as one connection (one service entry point). CDB/PDB environments count as one connection per CDB.
Annual billing gives you 20% off the monthly rate — equivalent to 2.4 months free per year. Individual drops from $49 to $39/conn/mo. Team from $39/$29 to $31/$23. Business from $29/$19 to $23/$15.
When you reach your tier's connection cap (5 for Individual, 25 for Team, 50 for Business), you can't add more without upgrading. Existing connections and health checks continue running normally. You'll see an upgrade prompt on the dashboard.
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period. Manage your plan at Settings → Billing.
Every new account gets 5 free health checks per company. Run the full diagnostic suite, get AI summaries, explore demo mode — all before paying. When you're ready to monitor continuously, pick a plan.
Yes. Every tier gets the full 200+ check catalog including all EBS checks (Concurrent Manager, Workflow Mailer, ADOP, Apps Tier). Tiers differ by connections, seats, and governance features — not check coverage.
