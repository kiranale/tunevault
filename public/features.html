Autonomous Monitoring & Email Alerts

Schedule health checks on a cadence (hourly, daily, weekly) and receive email alerts the moment new critical or warning findings are detected. Delta-aware logic suppresses noise — you only get alerted when the finding is new or changes severity, not on every run.

Live shipped
MON01Scheduled health checks

Configure cadence per connection: every N minutes, daily, or weekly. Runs automatically in the background.
