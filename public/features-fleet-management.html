Capabilities

Built for teams managing multiple Oracle environments

Whether you manage 3 databases or 30, Fleet Management scales your visibility without scaling your workload.

🎯

Health Score Aggregation

Every connection's latest health check score displayed in a sortable, filterable table. Worst scores float to the top automatically.

🔔

Finding Severity Breakdown

Critical, warning, and informational finding counts per connection. See at a glance which instances have active issues requiring attention.

🕒

Autonomous Monitoring Status

See which connections have scheduled monitoring active, their cadence, last run timestamp, and next scheduled check — all in one row.

🔌

DB + EBS Unified

Database-only and EBS connections appear in the same fleet view. EBS instances show both DB-level and EBS-level health indicators side by side.

📨

Email Alert Integration

Fleet overview connects to autonomous monitoring. Delta-aware email alerts fire only on new or worsened findings — no duplicate noise from stable issues.

📋

One-Click Deep Dive

Click any connection row to jump directly to its latest health check report. No navigating menus — go from fleet view to full diagnostics instantly.

Use cases

Who needs Fleet Management?

Any DBA team managing more than one Oracle environment benefits from a unified health view.

Production DBA

Morning health check in 30 seconds

Open Fleet, scan scores. Green across the board? Move on. Red on PROD-02? Click through, see the 2 critical findings, act. No logging into 8 servers individually.

DBA Manager

Team standup without surprises

Share the fleet view in standup. Everyone sees the same scores. Assign the critical findings to the right DBA. Track resolution across check runs.

EBS Admin

DB + EBS in one view

EBS environments have both database-level and application-level health concerns. Fleet shows both in one row — no switching between DB monitoring and EBS operations.

Managed Service Provider

Multi-client monitoring

Each client's Oracle instances appear in your fleet. Monitor health across all clients from a single dashboard. Export per-client reports for SLA compliance.
